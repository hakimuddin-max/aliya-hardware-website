About Us

A Formidable Repute Built Over 35 Years

ALIYA HARDWARE LLC was established in 1989 and has grown into one of the UAE's most trusted names in industrial, oilfield, and marine supply. Headquartered on First Industrial Street (BMW Road) in Sharjah, we have served clients across the UAE and broader Middle East for over three decades.

As a specialist supplier of equipment and spares to the industrial, oilfield, and marine sectors, we offer technologically superior and cost-effective turnkey procurement solutions.
