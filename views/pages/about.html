Our Journey

Built from the Ground Up

ALIYA HARDWARE LLC was established in 1989 by a team of dedicated procurement professionals who saw the growing demand for reliable industrial supply in the UAE. Starting from First Industrial Street in Sharjah — still our home today — we have grown with the region, serving the oilfield, marine, construction, and manufacturing sectors across the UAE and Middle East.

Over 35 years, we have built an extensive global supplier network, a deep understanding of Middle East logistics, and a reputation for getting it right — every time. Our clients range from global energy companies to local contractors, all served with the same commitment to quality and timeliness.

Today, ALIYA HARDWARE LLC remains proudly headquartered in Sharjah, UAE, continuing to serve as a dependable, trusted procurement partner for the region.
